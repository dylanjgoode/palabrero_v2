Overall Style and Visual Feel

The site presents a professional, minimalist design. It uses a clean layout with a clear headline, and distinct sections for key area. There is modest use of imagery and visual assets, so text and structure dominate the user experience.

The overall impression is serious, academic, and restrained.

Written language on the site is formal and informational. It emphasizes authority and expertise. The visuals do not distract; they support factual communication.

The color palette (based on default text and perhaps restrained use of accent colors) feels sober, aimed at a professional audience rather than broad entertainment.
